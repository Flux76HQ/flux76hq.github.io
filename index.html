The idea

You run it. You own it.

Flux76 builds self-hosted media & utility apps for people who like to keep their data close. No accounts you don't control, no surprise cloud sync — you run the backend, the apps talk to it, and everything stays on your infrastructure. Native where it counts, a PWA where you want reach, and a clean Docker backend underneath.

Philosophy

Built to last, not to rent.

01 · Self-hosted first

Your data lives on your hardware — Unraid, Docker, a Raspberry Pi, whatever you like.

02 · Privacy by default

No tracking, no data selling, no surprise cloud sync. Ever.

03 · Native + web

SwiftUI apps for iOS, PWAs for everywhere else, one backend behind them.

04 · Long-term thinking

Year-versioned releases and clear upgrade paths — software you can keep.
